What is Liquidity in Simple words?

Liquidity = Clusters of people's
            stop losses and orders
            sitting at certain prices

Think of it like this:

Imagine a street with 100 shops
All shops keep cash in same locker
If someone knows where locker is
They go there first to grab cash

In trading:
Big players (whales) KNOW where
retail traders put their stop losses
They go there FIRST
Grab that liquidity
Then reverse direction

----

🎯 WHAT IS A LIQUIDITY SWEEP?

Liquidity Sweep = Big player pushes price
                  BEYOND a key level
                  Triggers everyone's stops
                  Then REVERSES direction fast

It looks like:
Price breaks support → Looks like crash
Everyone's stops trigger → They get sold out
Price immediately reverses UP
Retail traders watch in shock
"Why did it go down then come back up?!"

Answer: You just got swept.
Your stop loss = Their entry.

----

4. Whales collected that liquidity
5. Price shot back up fast
6. Retail traders sold at bottom
7. Whales bought at bottom

✅ WHAT IS CONFIRMATION ENTRY?

Most beginners make this mistake:

They see price sweep below support
They think "Oh it's going up now!"
They BUY immediately during sweep

Result: They buy during the drop
Price goes lower first
They panic and sell

SMART entry = Wait for CONFIRMATION
= Wait for PROOF that reversal happened
= Don't catch the falling knife

-----

Step 1 — Identify the Sweep

Watch for price to:
→ Break BELOW obvious support
→ OR break ABOVE obvious resistance

Key: The break must look sharp and fast
     Like a spike or wick on candle
     Not a slow gradual move

On chart it looks like:
A long candle wick poking below support
Then immediately closing back above

This wick = The sweep


Step 2 — Wait For Rejection Candle

After the sweep look for:

BULLISH REJECTION (for buy entry):
→ Big red candle goes below support
→ Next candle is GREEN
→ Green candle closes ABOVE support level
→ This proves buyers stepped in

Candle pattern looks like:
   |          ← Long wick below (sweep)
   |
[GREEN]       ← Confirmation candle
   |
This = "Bullish engulfing after sweep"
Powerful buy signal ✅
