What is Liquidity in Simple words?

Liquidity = Clusters of people's
            stop losses and orders
            sitting at certain prices

Think of it like this:

Imagine a street with 100 shops
All shops keep cash in same locker
If someone knows where locker is
They go there first to grab cash

In trading:
Big players (whales) KNOW where
retail traders put their stop losses
They go there FIRST
Grab that liquidity
Then reverse direction

----

🎯 WHAT IS A LIQUIDITY SWEEP?

Liquidity Sweep = Big player pushes price
                  BEYOND a key level
                  Triggers everyone's stops
                  Then REVERSES direction fast

It looks like:
Price breaks support → Looks like crash
Everyone's stops trigger → They get sold out
Price immediately reverses UP
Retail traders watch in shock
"Why did it go down then come back up?!"

Answer: You just got swept.
Your stop loss = Their entry.

----

📈 VISUAL EXAMPLE
Liquidity Sweep Below Support

Price chart looks like this:

$89 ─────────────────────────
$88 ──────╮
$87 ──────╯╲
$86 ────────╲────╮
$85 ─────────────╯╲          ← Support level
$84.50 ──────────── ╲        ← EVERYONE'S STOPS HERE
$84.20 ──────────────╲╱      ← SWEEP HAPPENS HERE
$85 ──────────────────╲╱╲    ← Price reverses UP fast
$86 ───────────────────────╱
$87 ──────────────────────╱
$88 ─────────────────────╱   ← Big move up begins

What happened:
1. Price fell to $85 support
2. Broke below to $84.20 (swept stops)
3. All retail stop losses triggered
4. Whales collected that liquidity
5. Price shot back up fast
6. Retail traders sold at bottom
7. Whales bought at bottom
